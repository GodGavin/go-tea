A Server-Driven Animation

A 30fps bouncing ball animation being driven entirely by the server. You'd probably never want to do this, but is fun to know that you can! Clicking 'start' fires off a never-ending sequence of messages with a 33ms delay between each. Each message sets the x and y coordinates of the ball and the scene is rerendered by the Gotea runtime.
